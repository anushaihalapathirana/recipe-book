# Apple crisp recipe

## Ingredients

- **6 cups** peeled and sliced apples (Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or your favorite baking apples)
- **2 tablespoons** lemon juice
- **1/2 cup** granulated sugar
- **1/2 teaspoon** ground cinnamon
- **1/4 teaspoon** ground nutmeg
- **1 pinch** salt

Topping:

- **3/4 cup** old-fashioned rolled oats
- **3/4 cup** all-purpose flour
- **1/2 cup** packed brown sugar
- **1/2 teaspoon** ground cinnamon
- **1/4 teaspoon** salt
- **1/2 cup** cold unsalted butter, cubed

## Instructions

1. **Preheat Oven:**
   - Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. **Prepare Filling:**
   - In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with lemon juice, granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
   - Transfer apple mixture to a 9x9-inch baking dish or similar size oven-safe dish.

3. **Make Topping:**
   - In another bowl, combine rolled oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
   - Add cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or fingers to mix until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

4. **Assemble and Bake:**
   - Sprinkle the topping evenly over the apple mixture.
   - Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apple filling is bubbling.

5. **Serve:**
   - Allow the crisp to cool slightly for 10-15 minutes.
   - Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream if desired.
