# Chocolate mousse recipe

## Ingredients

- **6 oz** semisweet chocolate, chopped
- **3 tablespoons** unsalted butter
- **3** large eggs, separated
- **1/4 cup** granulated sugar
- **1 cup** heavy cream
- **1 teaspoon** vanilla extract
- **Pinch** salt

## Instructions

1. **Melt Chocolate:**
   - In a heatproof bowl over simmering water (double boiler), melt the chocolate and butter together, stirring until smooth.
   - Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

2. **Prepare Egg Yolks:**
   - Stir egg yolks into the melted chocolate until fully combined.

3. **Whip Egg Whites:**
   - In a clean bowl, beat egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form.
   - Gradually add granulated sugar, continuing to beat until glossy stiff peaks form.

4. **Whip Cream:**
   - In another bowl, whip the heavy cream with vanilla extract until medium peaks form.

5. **Fold Mixtures:**
   - Gently fold whipped cream into chocolate mixture until combined.
   - Fold in egg whites in batches, keeping as much air as possible.

6. **Chill:**
   - Divide mousse into serving cups and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or overnight) to set.

7. **Serve:**
   - Garnish with chocolate shavings or fresh berries if desired.
   - Serve chilled.
