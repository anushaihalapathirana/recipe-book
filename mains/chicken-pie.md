# Chicken pie recipe

## Ingredients

- **2 cups** cooked chicken, shredded (rotisserie or poached breast)
- **1** store-bought pie crust (or homemade double crust)
- **1 cup** frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
- **1/2 cup** onion, diced
- **2 cloves** garlic, minced
- **1/3 cup** butter
- **1/3 cup** all-purpose flour
- **1 3/4 cups** chicken broth
- **2/3 cup** whole milk (or half-and-half)
- **1 teaspoon** dried thyme
- **1/2 teaspoon** dried rosemary
- **Salt and pepper** to taste
- **1** egg (beaten, for egg wash)

## Instructions

1. **Preheat Oven:**
   - Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. **Prepare Filling:**
   - In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
   - Add the minced garlic and cook another 30 seconds until fragrant.
   - Stir in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
   - Gradually whisk in chicken broth and milk until smooth.
   - Add dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper, and simmer until the mixture thickens (about 3-5 minutes).
   - Stir in the cooked chicken and frozen mixed vegetables; cook until the vegetables are heated through. Remove from heat.

3. **Assemble Pie:**
   - Line a 9-inch pie dish with one pie crust.
   - Pour chicken filling into the crust, spreading it evenly.
   - Cover with second crust, trim excess, and crimp edges to seal.
   - Cut a few slits in the top crust to vent steam.
   - Brush the top with beaten egg for a golden finish.

4. **Bake:**
   - Place the pie on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 35-45 minutes or until crust is golden brown and filling bubbles.
   - If edges brown too quickly, cover them with foil.

5. **Serve:**
   - Let the chicken pie rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
   - Serve warm with a simple side salad or steamed greens.
